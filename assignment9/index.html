ROW SCRABBLE

Created by Hillary Ssemakula
RULES

  1. The game has a tile rack and a scrabble board: Your goal is to take as many tiles from the rack as possible and put each on a slot on the scrabble board to create a valid word. There are 7 tiles and 7 slots on the board.

  2. The game instantly checks the validity of the words that you spell out on the board. These words have to have atleast 2 letters. When you are ready, you can submit the word by clicking the "NEXT WORD" button. Your word is stored, the tiles on the board are taken and the tile rack is refilled to have 7 tiles again. The tile bag(not shown here) has 100 tiles.

  3. In the event that you get stuck, you can place a tile back on the tile rack and swap it out for another tile. However, originally blank tiles cannot be returned to the rack.

  4. When you click swap, all tiles on the rack will be swapped out for other tiles. You cannot swap out tiles more than 3 times before submitting atleast one valid word.

  5. When you submit an invalid word, the game will ask you if you want to GIVE UP If you click the "GIVE UP" button, you'll be shown your total score and the game will reset.

  6. When you submit over 5 invalid words, the game will show an option to "GIVE UP". The game ends when you either submit all tiles as words or "give up".

Hint: State initials count as words
